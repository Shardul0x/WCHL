Secure Your Genius.
On-Chain.

ProofMint is a decentralized intellectual property vault, anchoring your ideas to the Internet Computer blockchain for permanent, tamper-proof proof of authorship.

Core Features

Blockchain Timestamping

Generates a SHA-256 hash of your idea and anchors it immutably on the Internet Computer, creating a permanent record of creation.

Secure Authentication

Leverages Internet Identity for passwordless, biometrically-secure login, linking all IP to a unique, cryptographic Principal ID.

Verifiable Certificate

Generate and download a proof-of-authorship certificate at any time, containing all cryptographic details needed to prove your claim.

Team Collaboration

Securely add team members to your private projects using their Principal IDs, allowing for controlled, collaborative development.

AI Originality Engine

Our on-chain AI compares semantic meaning, not just words, to detect conceptual plagiarism and ensure your ideas are truly unique.

DAO Governance

A community-driven moderation system allows for decentralized dispute resolution, voting, and reputation management.
